Lasaanga Recipie

who wouldnt want a delicious lasagna after work?
Warm and reusable!
below is a lovely lasagna recipie:

Ingredients:

  • creamy ricotta mixture
  • savory meat sauce
  • lots of mozzarella cheese

How to make:

Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish. Add 4
boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles). Spread 1/3 of
the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce. Repeat the
same layers two more times.
